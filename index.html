Frequently asked questions.

What are AI agents, and how do they work in a yard management system?

AI agents are intelligent, task-focused modules that operate autonomously or in coordination to optimize specific operations. In our yard management system, these agents handle tasks like cargo allocation, gate-in scheduling, real-time monitoring, and more. Each agent learns from data and improves decision-making, helping the system run more efficiently without manual intervention.

How does the AI system handle real-time yard status updates?

The "Current Yard Status" module uses real-time data streams from sensors, RFID tags, and IoT devices to monitor the movement and placement of containers. Our AI analyzes this data continuously and displays a live dashboard so operators always have a clear, accurate view of yard occupancy and traffic.

Can your AI modules optimize cargo planning and reallocation automatically?

Yes. The "Cargo Planning & Allocation" and "Cargo Reallocation" agents are built to evaluate current yard constraints, future bookings, and historical patterns. They suggest optimal container placements and reallocate cargo when disruptions occur—automatically or with human oversight, depending on your setup.

Is the port navigation AI capable of dynamic rerouting or real-time recommendations?

Absolutely. The "Port Navigation" agent provides real-time route suggestions to guide vehicles within the terminal efficiently. If congestion, blockages, or delays are detected, the AI dynamically reroutes vehicles to avoid bottlenecks and reduce turnaround time.

How does the system improve operational efficiency compared to traditional methods?

By automating repetitive decision-making, reducing errors, and analyzing massive datasets instantly, our AI modules minimize downtime, optimize space utilization, and boost throughput. Many clients report 20–30% efficiency gains within the first few months of deployment.

Is the AI-based yard system customizable for different port or terminal layouts?

Yes, our system is fully modular and can adapt to varying layouts, infrastructure, and operational workflows. The AI agents are trained to learn from your environment, and the configuration layer allows easy customization to match your yard’s specific needs.
